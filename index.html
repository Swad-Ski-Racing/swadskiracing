ABOUT US

Swad Ski Racing is a premier ski racing club dedicated to nurturing talent and fostering a passion for alpine skiing. Our mission is to provide top-tier coaching and training programs that cater to athletes of all ages and skill levels, from beginners to seasoned competitors.

Find Out More

TRAINING

Swad Ski Racing will not just be a time slot on a local dry slope where members and non members can turn up when they like and get coached on an adhoc basis. From developing novice skiers through to continual personal development of athletes who have been with the club for a number of years, a structured weekly coaching plan, coupled with rewards and club activities will ensure the longevity of SSR for many years to come. COACHES

Russ Pike

Head Coach
A Snowsport England Accredited Coach working toward Level 2, I reignited my passion for skiing after 20 years away. From launching a school team to coaching university racers, I’m now proud to help grow ski racing in the East Midlands with Swadlincote Snowsport Centre.

Matt O'Neill

Assistant Coach
Matt is a highly experienced ski racing coach with over five years of coaching and a decade of broader sports expertise. Based in Gloucester, he has coached for various clubs and institutions and remains dedicated to developing skiers at all levels, even while spending time in the Alps.

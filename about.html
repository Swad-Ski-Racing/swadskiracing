ABOUT US

Swad Ski Racing is a premier ski racing club dedicated to nurturing talent and fostering a passion for alpine skiing. Our mission is to provide top-tier coaching and training programs that cater to athletes of all ages and skill levels, from beginners to seasoned competitors.

Our team of experienced coaches employs a comprehensive approach, focusing on technical skills, physical conditioning, and mental preparation. We believe in creating a supportive and challenging environment that encourages athletes to reach their full potential.

Located in the heart of the swadlincote Alps, our facilities offer the ideal place to race train in the uk, providing an ideal setting for rigorous training and development. We are committed to the growth of our athletes, emphasizing not only competitive success but also the development of lifelong skills such as discipline, resilience, and teamwork.

At Swad Ski Racing, we pride ourselves on our community-oriented approach, fostering a sense of camaraderie among our members. We organize regular events, workshops, and competitions to engage our athletes and their families, building a strong and supportive network.

Join us at Swad Ski Racing, where passion meets performance, and together, we carve the path to excellence in alpine skiing.
